Well, here's a janitor's job open to you.

JUDGE.

[_Sighing, and turning over the leaves of the ledger, then hopefully._]

Here's the very thing for you, then--postman in a rural district.

JUDGE.

[_Closing ledger with a bang, and rising._]

Well, you're too particular, and I have not time to bother with you. I bid you good after----

IMP.

[_Slides from his desk, runs to railing, and speaks suavely._]
